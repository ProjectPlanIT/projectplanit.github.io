About Plan-It

Plan-IT is a platform for travellers to browse for itinerary templates to use or request a customised itinerary from planners. These planners are unlike the itinerary planning websites, they are not automated systems but actual people that would join Plan-IT to help plan and customise itineraries for these travellers.
