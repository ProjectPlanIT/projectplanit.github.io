Our Business Model

Plan-It is a social media platform that connects travellers and planners together.

Planners would need put in a deposit of S$5 during the creation of their itinerary. Planners are able to put in content and set the itinerary price.
A request will be sent to 5 anonymous high rating planners to verify your itinerary. Your S$5 deposit will be distributed to these 5 itinerary verifiers. If 3 or approve your itinerary, then your itinerary will be listed on our platform for travellers to preview. However, if 3 or more itinerary planner decline your itinerary, it will not be listed on Plan It Platform.
